COCαDA

The Interatomic Contact Database & tool

COCαDA-web is a tool and a database that presents contacts in all structures in the PDB database. COCαDA calculates seven types of contacts: hydrogen bonds, hydrophobic, aromatic attractive, repulsive, salt bridges and disulfide bridges.
